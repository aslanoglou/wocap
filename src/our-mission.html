Crafting the future of Working Capital

Our mission is simple: to transform every invoice into an opportunity for growth, liquidity, and resilience-at scale"

WoCap is reimagining the future of corporate finance by unlocking the untapped power of working capital. Our mission is simple: to transform every invoice into an opportunity for growth, liquidity, and resilience—at scale.

Through our intelligent dynamic discounting platform, we enable enterprises to optimize early payments in a way that benefits both buyers and suppliers. By creating smart, real-time incentives across the supply chain, WoCap helps businesses unlock value hidden in plain sight—fueling healthier cash flow and stronger partnerships.

WoCap is the go-to platform addressing Working Capital challenges for all Suppliers, irrespective of company size or volume of invoices. It is available to all and eliminates uncomfortable negotiations, high prices dictated by financial institutions, complicated onboarding requirements and paperwork. We have a deep understanding of all corporate needs and our vision is to always be one step ahead, before they arise.

Through our digital platform, fostering an automated Working Capital solution, we strive to make your company’s financial planning easier and powerful, enabling you to address market headwinds. Built for the modern CFO, WoCap brings clarity, control, and automation to a space long overdue for innovation. We are redefining working capital as a strategic lever—not just a line item—helping companies shift from reactive cash management to proactive financial leadership.

OUR MISSION

Revolutionizing Buyer-Supplier Relationships

Our mission is simple.

  • Streamline the relationship between Buyers and their Suppliers and unlock Working Capital for companies that really need it.
  • Help companies grow and create a healthier economy for everyone.
  • Use the latest sophisticated technological tools and together with full transparency provide all companies with the most fair capital allocation.
  • Retain our flexibility, innovate and always adopt latest market trends in our client approach and product spectrum.

We believe in creating win-win solutions for Buyers and their Suppliers

Our platform empowers Buyers to reduce costs while enabling Suppliers access Working Capital faster. By running competitive auctions, we ensure transparency, fairness and maximum value for all parties.
