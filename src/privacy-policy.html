Wocap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 02, 2025

  1. 1. Introduction

    Wocap is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Greek law.

  2. 2. Data Controller

    Wocap S.A., based in Athens, is the data controller responsible for processing your personal data.

  3. 3. What We Collect

    • Business Contact Information: Name, company, position, email, and phone number
    • Account Data: Login credentials, ERP integration data
    • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, session data, cookies

  4. 4. Legal Basis for Processing

    We process your data based on:

    • Contractual necessity (e.g., providing services)
    • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving the platform)
    • Legal obligations (e.g., AML compliance)
    • Consent (e.g., for marketing communications)

  5. 5. How We Use Your Data

    • To onboard and support users
    • To operate and improve the platform
    • To communicate updates and service-related notices
    • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

  6. 6. Data Sharing

    We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, email systems). These providers process data on our behalf under strict data protection terms. We do not sell or trade your personal data.

  7. 7. International Data Transfers

    Data may be transferred outside the EEA under appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

  8. 8. Data Retention

    We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by applicable law.

  9. 9. Your Rights

    Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

    • - Access your data
    • Request correction or deletion
    • Object to or restrict processing
    • Withdraw consent
    • File a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (www.dpa.gr)

  10. 10. Cookies

    We use cookies to improve user experience and analytics. Users can manage cookie preferences via browser settings or through our cookie banner.

  11. 11. Security Measures

    We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  12. 12. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

    Email: info@wocap.io

    Address: Wocap S.A., Evryalis 14A, 14562 Athens, Greece
